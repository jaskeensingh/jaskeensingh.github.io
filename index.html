About Me

Hi I'm Jaskeen Singh. I currently work as an AI and Data Analyst in Deloitte USI. From generative AI and mathematics to the mysteries of quantum physics and astronomy, I'm driven by an insatiable curiosity to understand the world and create solutions that matter. When I'm not decoding complex datasets, you'll find me on the squash court, exploring new running trails, or sketching my way through creative insights. My passion lies in building technology that can genuinely help people at large, bridging the gap between intellectual exploration and practical impact. Travel, continuous learning, and a deep love for understanding the universe fuel my journey. Always eager to connect, collaborate, and turn wild ideas into meaningful innovations.
